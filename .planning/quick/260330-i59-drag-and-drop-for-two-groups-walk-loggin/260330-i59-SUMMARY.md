### Task 2 ŌĆö WalkLogSheet DnD Two Groups

Replaced the confusing click-cycle UX (unassigned ŌåÆ A ŌåÆ B ŌåÆ remove) with drag-and-drop:

- **`DraggableChip`** ŌĆö module-level sub-component wrapping any pool or group chip via `useDraggable`. Includes an optional `onRemove` prop that renders an X button; X uses `onPointerDown` stopPropagation to prevent drag initiation on click.
- **`DroppableBox`** ŌĆö module-level sub-component wrapping any container via `useDroppable`. Applies a per-group ring colour on hover: blue-400 for Group A, amber-400 for Group B, slate-300 for pool.
- **`handleDragEnd`** ŌĆö routes drop events: `group-a` assigns dog to A, `group-b` assigns to B, `pool` removes assignment. Clears validation errors on valid drops.
- **`DragOverlay`** ŌĆö floating chip label displayed during drag for clear visual feedback.
- **Removed** `handlePoolDogClick` entirely ŌĆö click-cycle is gone.
- Pool label updated to "Drag dogs into Group A or Group B".
